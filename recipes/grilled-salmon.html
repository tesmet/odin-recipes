Grilled Salmon

Decription

Grilled salmon is healthy and easy to prepare on a sizzling hot barbecue. Learn the essential tips for nailing beautiful char marks and achieving a tender, flaky texture. It’s a seafood dish that requires only a few simple seasonings and cooks in less than 10 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 2 Salmon Fillets
  • Paper towels
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Cut the salmon into 2 ½ to 3-inch fillets.
  2. Wrap in paper towels and refrigerate for 15 minutes.
  3. Brush each side with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
  4. Preheat gas grill to high, clean and oil the grates.
  5. Place fish on a diagonal, skin-side up. Cover and cook about 4 to 5 minutes.
  6. Flip, cover and cook until the desired doneness is reached.
  7. Serve with lemon wedges.
