Mashed Potatoes

Description

These creamy, buttery, mashed potatoes are the perfect side dish for turkey, roast beef, or pork chops. In just a few simple steps, you’ll have delicious mashed potatoes for Thanksgiving, Sunday dinner, or just a side to weeknight meatloaf.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Sour Cream
  • Butter
  • Milk
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. Fill a large pot with 2 inches of water and salt.
  2. Set steamer basket in pot, making sure water doesn’t come through the holes.
  3. Bring to a boil, then reduce to a rapid simmer.
  4. Add potatoes to steamer basket, cover pot, and steam for 25 minutes or until tender.
